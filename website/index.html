Our Vision


Budget Buddy is a app we created to help students achieve financial freedom At Budget Buddy, our vision is to empower students with the financial tools they need to succeed. We believe that every student should have access to financial education and resources to help them achieve their goals. Our vision is to create a community of financially savvy students who are equipped to make smart financial decisions both during and after college. We believe that by providing the right tools and resources, we can help students build a strong financial foundation for their future.
